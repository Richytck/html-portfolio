The Best Movies According to Richard

My top 3 movies of all-time.

John Wick

Best action film ever made. Loved every part of the film. Keanu Reeves is an absolute charm of a actor.

Interview with the Vampire

Top vampire film. Garbed my attention to to very end.

The Green Mile

Super drama that made you feel along the characters in this movie. Sad ending but very interesting to watch.
